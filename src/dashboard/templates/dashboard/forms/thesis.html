CALL FOR APPLICANTS

UG-BANGA-3 THESIS COMPLETION GRANTS

In line with our mandate to enhance the quality and timely completion of PhD education in West Africa, the Building A New Generation of Academics in Africa (BANGA-Africa) Project Phase 3, is offering a limited number of grants to third and final year PhD students of the University of Ghana for the completion of their PhD studies

Eligible expenses covered by the grant are:

  • Final year school fees
  • Research costs (to finalise data collection)
  • Thesis production costs (printing, binding, photocopying)
  • Thesis proofreading and editing

APPLICATION PROCEDURE

To apply, interested candidates should submit the following Documents:

  • A completed application form
  • A maximum 3-page research proposal clearly outlining the background, objectives, scope, methodology and status of PhD completion with proof where possible.
  • A copy of your admission letter
  • Most recent CV
NB:
Preference will be given to students whose studies are not funded by their employers or other donors.
This grant is open to Ghanaian nationals only.

For further enquiries, you may send an email to the Assistant Registrar at banga-africa@ug.edu.gh or call 0558182449
